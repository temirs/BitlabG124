Khabib to Connor: it's the same thing

Conor McGregor and Khabib Nurmagomedov finally came face to face in New York as they took part in a press conference ahead of their October 6 title fight. The two men will clash in Las Vegas next month when the lightweight belt will be on the line at the T-Mobile Arena. It will be the first time McGregor will walk to the Octagon in almost two years since he knocked out Eddie Alvarez to win the 155lb belt. Since then he has been stripped of both the featherweight and lightweight belts but he also traded punches with Floyd Mayweather in the boxing ring before losing by 10th-round stoppage 13 months ago. In the interim period, Nurmagomedov beat Al Iaquinta to win the vacant belt and will make his first defence against the Irishman in just over two weeks' time.
